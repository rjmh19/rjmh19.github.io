Phase 1 — supine
1
Knee-to-chest pull
supine · both sides
90s
hips
Lie flat on your back. Draw one knee slowly to your chest, hold 3 seconds, release. Alternate legs. 8 slow repetitions per side. Keep the opposite leg flat. The therapeutic value comes from the hold, not the speed.
Wrist safe — no hand loading
2
Supine hip circles core
supine · both sides
90s
hips
Lie on back, draw one knee to 90 degrees. Slow circles with the knee — 6 forward, 6 backward. Imagine drawing a large circle on the ceiling. Primary hip mobility exercise. Switch sides.
Wrist safe — no hand loading
3
Supine spinal rotation core
supine · both sides
90s
spine
Lie on back, knees bent. Let both knees slowly fall to one side — pause, breathe, return. 6 per side, very slow. Arms out for counterbalance. Let gravity do the work — do not force range.
Wrist safe — no hand loading
4
Ankle circles + foot pumps
supine · both feet
60s
ankles
Still lying down. Flex and point each foot 10 times, then circle each ankle 8 times each direction. Both feet simultaneously. Important for gout management — uric acid pools in distal joints overnight.
Wrist safe — no hand loading
Phase 2 — forearms on floor
5
Cat-cow on forearms core
kneeling · forearms flat
90s
spine
Kneel, forearms flat on floor, elbows under shoulders — zero wrist load. Arch spine up (cat), hold 2 sec, sag down (cow), hold 2 sec. 10 slow cycles. Exhale on cat, inhale on cow.
Forearms only — zero wrist or thumb load
6
Thread-the-needle core
forearms on floor · both sides
90s
rhomboid
From forearms position, lift one arm and slide it along the floor under your body, rotating the thoracic spine. Let shoulder drop toward the floor. Hold 3 seconds. 6 per side. Primary rhomboid exercise.
Sliding arm — back of hand or fist, no thumb pressure

Feet shoulder-width, hands on hips. Large slow pelvis circles — 8 forward, 8 backward. Exaggerate the movement. Knees soft. Joints are now ready for weight-bearing after supine work.
Hands on hips — no wrist involvement

Slow shoulder rolls: 8 forward, 8 backward — large and deliberate. Cross-body arm swings x10. 6 full arm circles each direction. Fists loosely closed.

9
Knee circles + calf raises core
standing · palms on knees
90s
knees
Feet together, slight squat, palms on knees. Slow circles — 8 each direction. Then stand and do 15 slow calf raises, 1 second hold at top. Critical before running.
Palms resting flat — minimal thumb pressure
10
Wrist + hand mobility
standing · always last
60s
wrist
Arms in front, palms down. Wrist circles — 8 each direction. Open and close hands 10 times. Thumb circles — 6 each direction, pain-free range only. Stop immediately if sharp pain. Always last — never do this cold.
Pain-free range only — stop if sharp pain
Core routine (exercises marked core — approx 10 min): use on good stiffness days or when time is short. Full routine on moderate or bad stiffness days.
Not a stretching routine — a therapeutic mobility routine. Slow, controlled, circular movement distributes synovial fluid. Never force range. Always supine first, then forearms, then standing.
